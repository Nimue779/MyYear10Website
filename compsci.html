In computer science I learned about binary and hexadecimal, which is base 2 and base 16 respectively. Base 2 has only 2 different digits, and base 16 has 16 different digits. Computers store every piece of information in binary, so the computer is made up of zeros and ones. Hexadecimal is a compact way of representing binary.
